ABOUT

Los Angeles based sketch-comedy writer, actor, and stand-up comic. After studying at The Groundlings (the legendary Los Angeles improv company that spawned the careers of stars like Will Ferrell, Jon Lovitz, Phil Hartman and Lisa Kudrow) I went on to work with Fox Digital on "The Chad Ridgely Show", a MADtv / Saturday Night Live style comedy show (the name was their idea, I swear.) After cranking out such viral web hits like the "Gay or Not Gay Game Show" and "Healthy Tips" as well as my popular spoofs of Chris Hansen's MSNBC show "To Catch a Predator," I've recently been creating comedy music videos like the catchy "Hot Lesbians Makin' Out & Smokin' While Tied Up & Fightin'" and its follow up song "Big Boobies on Blondes or Brunettes or Redheads." For more personalized bad jokes, I appear regularly on the L.A. comedy club circuit and can be seen performing at the world-famous Ice House Comedy Club in Pasadena and Flappers Comedy Club in Burbank. I also do nightly shows in front of my bedroom mirror.
